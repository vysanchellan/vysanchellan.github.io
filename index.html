FAQ

Quick answers.

How long does a website take?

Most Launch sites go live in 5–7 days. Authority sites take 10–14 days. Concierge typically takes 3 weeks. I always commit to a date in the proposal.

Do I own the website?

Yes — 100%. The domain stays in your name, the code is yours, and I'll hand over every credential. No lock-in, ever.

Do you do hosting and maintenance?

Yes. Hosting is R250/month including SSL, backups, and small content updates. You can also self-host — I'll set it up either way.

Can you integrate real booking systems?

Absolutely. I integrate Calendly, Fresha, Google Calendar, or build a custom booking system that posts straight to your WhatsApp and email.

Do you only work in healthcare?

No — anything booking-driven works: salons, barbers, gyms, optometrists, dentists, pharmacies, mobile mechanics, tutors, photographers. The framework is the same.

How do payments work?

50% to start, 50% on launch. EFT or PayShap. Invoices in ZAR. No subscriptions unless you opt in to maintenance.
